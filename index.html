I'm a data engineer building pipelines, warehouses, and analytics infrastructure. Currently at TouchNote in the UK, working across Airflow, dbt, Redshift, and Looker.

Before that, I worked as a data engineer at Open Financial Technologies and as a data analyst at Mojocare and Eloelo. I'm interested in the full journey of data — ingestion, modeling, governance, and the downstream decisions it enables.

I also write about data engineering, machine learning, and the craft of working with data.
